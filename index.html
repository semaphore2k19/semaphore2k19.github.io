About Semaphore '19
Semaphore is a two day South India Level technical fest, conveyed and organised by Department of MCA, NMAMIT, Nitte. Over the years it has evolved into a platform that provides plethora of opportunities to tech lovers to explore their knowledge, creativity, and talents. With all fun, entertaining and brainstorming events, Semaphore for the past 18 years has served as an ideal milestone to experience the beauty of information technology. And we are here, once again, continuing the legacy with the 19th edition of our fest. So set yourself, get ready and join us at SEMAPHORE '19

General Rules

The Twist in the Story.
  • 1) Registration fee ₹1,000/- per team.
  • 2) Maximum of 12 participants are allowed from each team.
  • 3) Participating teams must have to report at the reception desk by 9.00 am.
  • 4) College ID and permission letter from college is mandatory.
  • 5) Teams must participate in all the events in order to qualify for the overall championship.
  • 6) Rounds of all the events will be disclosed on the day of the fest.
  • 7) Replacement of any participant of a team is not allowed after the registration.
  • 8) Participation teams should not reveal their college identity during the events.
  • 9) Decisions of organisers/ panel of judges will be final in all the events.
  • 10) Accommodation facilities will be arranged only upon prior notification.
  • 11) Change in rules related to any event, if any, will be highlighted on the official website and android app of SEMAPHORE '19.
  • 12) Formation of teams for some events must match the Schedule
